## Main responsibilities

### Project setup and coordination

At the start of the project, I helped establish the team structure, communication channels, and initial planning direction.

This included:

1. Setting up communication channels for the team.
2. Supporting role negotiation and role confirmation.
3. Helping establish the initial documentation and planning structure.
4. Coordinating early project artefacts and expectations.

### Sprint planning and delivery alignment

During the project, I helped maintain the sprint planning process and kept the team aligned with the required weekly outcomes.

This included:

1. Updating sprint planning evidence.
2. Communicating task changes to team members.
3. Supporting weekly task negotiation.
4. Helping clarify what needed to be completed during each sprint.
5. Checking that sprint work stayed aligned with the project requirements.

### Team communication

I helped guide team communication toward agreed project milestones and delivery goals.

This included:

1. Facilitating weekly team meetings.
2. Supporting discussion around task ownership and progress.
3. Helping team members understand current priorities.
4. Supporting coordination during peer-programming activities.

### Risk tracking

I maintained and summarised project risk evidence during the project.

This included tracking risks related to:

1. Schedule pressure.
2. Incomplete or changing specifications.
3. Team productivity.
4. Scope creep.
5. Team member availability.

The weekly risk material was later consolidated into a cleaned public `risk-summary.md` file for this portfolio version.

### Testing-related implementation

My direct testing-related contribution was implementation-focused rather than formal QA documentation.

I developed:

1. The automated JUnit 5 and Mockito test suite.
2. The k6 load testing script.

The Quality Manager led the broader quality assurance process, including user acceptance testing documentation and testing evidence. My automated test suite and k6 script supported that broader QA process.
